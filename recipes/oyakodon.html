Oyakodon (Chicken and Egg Bowl) Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for the traditional japanese staple meal, oyakodon. It should take no more than 25 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb chicken thigh
  • 1 onion
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup dashi
  • 2 cups cooked rice

Steps

  1. Thinly slice the onion.
  2. Prepare a clean pan.
  3. Add the soy sauce, mirin, sake, sugar, and dashi.
  4. Add a layer of the onions, then a layer of the sliced chicken
  5. Bring to a boil and brown the chicken.
  6. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Beat the eggs and pour over the chicken and onions.
  8. Cover and cook until the eggs are done.
  9. Serve over rice.
