Gyudon (Beef Bowl) Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for the traditional japanese staple meal, the beef bowl. It should take no more than 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb thinly sliced beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup dashi
  • 2 cups cooked rice

Steps

  1. Thinly slice the onion.
  2. Prepare a clean pan.
  3. Add the soy sauce, mirin, sake, sugar, and dashi.
  4. Add a layer of the onions, then a layer of the sliced beef
  5. Bring to a boil and brown the beef.
  6. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Serve over rice.
